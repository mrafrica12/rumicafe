Our Story

Coffee, but slower.

Rumi began with a simple wish — to build a corner of the city where people could sit without rushing. Where the coffee is made by someone who cares, the music doesn't fight you, and the chair is the kind you settle into.

Every cup carries a lineage. The beans come from the same continent that taught the world how to drink coffee — small farms, careful hands, generations of knowledge. We brew them slowly, on purpose.

We believe the most luxurious thing a café can offer isn't a fancy drink — it's the feeling of being unhurried.

— the Rumi teamNairobi · Est. 2024
The Space

Built for staying
a little longer.

Warm light, exposed brick, soft leather. A counter where someone will remember how you take it.

Rooted in Africa

Where every
ritual was born.

Coffee is not an invention. It is a memory. The first cup was poured in Ethiopia, shared between people sitting close, in no hurry to leave. That impulse — to brew something carefully and offer it generously — is what Rumi carries.

Our sourcing runs deep. The beans in your cup come from smallholder farms across East and West Africa, traded directly, roasted lightly, and served in a space that understands where they came from.

This is not origin story as marketing. It is obligation, and pleasure, and pride.
